About Blood Banking System?


We are trying to resolve the problem of blood emergencies by connecting
blood donors directly with the people when they are in need of the blood

Totally Free

Blood Bank's ultimate goal is to provide an easy -to-use, easy-to-access, fast, efficient, and reliable way to get life-saving blood, totally Free of cost.


Innovatory

Blood Bank is an innovative approach to address global health.We provide immediate access to blood donors.

Network Area

Blood Bank is one of several community organizations working together as a network that responds to emergencies in an efficient manner.

Get notified

Blood Bank works with networking partner in order to connect with blood donors and recipients through an automated SMS service and a mobile app.

What we will do?

We will connect blood donors with recipients, without any intermediary such as blood banks, for an efficient process.

Our super heroes

Volunteers are real super hero. Volunteers make up 96% of our total
 workforce and carry on our humanitarian work. Blood donation is healthy,
 our volunteers are available 24/7 to help and donate blood. Blood banks store blood
 bags but our volunteers are there with you in an emergency for a blood transfusion real time.
